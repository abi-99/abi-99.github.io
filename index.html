Cosmopolitan

We will avoid all Sex and the City references here, but the fact remains:
This cocktail is classic for a reason.

Ingredients
  • 2 oz Vodka
  • 1 oz Cointreau or Triple Sec
  • 1 oz Cranberry Juice
  • 1 oz Fresh Squeezed Lime
Preparation
  1. Combine vodka, cranberry juice, lime juice, and triple sec in a cocktail shaker. Fill shaker with ice, cover, and shake vigorously until outside of shaker is very cold, about 20 seconds.
  2. Strain cocktail through a Hawthorne strainer or a slotted spoon into a martini glass. Garnish with orange twist (or garnish of choice).

Whiskey Sour

The Whiskey Sour is a timeless classic cocktail.
If you need a reason to mix one up, National Whiskey Sour Day is August 25.

Ingredients
  • 2 oz Bourbon Whiskey
  • 1 ½ tbsp. Freshly Squeezed Lemon Juice
  • 1 ½ tbsp. Simple Syrup
  • ½ Orange Slice
  • 1 Maraschino Cherry
  • 1 egg white
Preparation
  1. Add the whiskey, lemon juice, simple syrup, and egg white to a cocktail shaker without ice. Shake for 15 seconds.
  2. Add ice to the cocktail shaker. Shake again for 30 seconds.
  3. Strain the drink into a glass; the foam will form a layer on the top. Serve with or without ice and garnish with the orange slice and cherry on a toothpick.

Mai Tai

Supposedly, the first person to try the cocktail was said to cry out “Mai Tai!”
Which means “the best"/ "out of this world” in Tahitian.

Ingredients
  • 1 ½ oz White Rum
  • ¾ oz Orange Curaçao
  • ¾ oz Freshly Squeezed Lime Juice
  • ½ oz Orgeat or Simple Syrup
  • ½ oz Dark Rum
Preparation
  1. Add the white rum, curaçao, lime juice and orgeat into a shaker with crushed ice and shake lightly (about 3 seconds).
  2. Pour into a double rocks glass.
  3. Float the dark rum over the top.
  4. Garnish with Fruits of Choice
